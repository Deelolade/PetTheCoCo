Benefits of Coconuts

  • Boosts immunity with antibacterial and antiviral properties.
  • Versatile uses in cooking, skincare, and hydration.
  • Supports energy levels and weight management.
  • May improve heart health by raising good HDL cholesterol.
  • Rich in healthy fats, fiber, and essential nutrients.

Risks of Coconuts

  • Potential for allergic reactions in sensitive individuals.
  • Excess saturated fats may affect cholesterol levels.
  • High in calories; overconsumption can lead to weight gain.
  • Can cause digestive issues for some people.
